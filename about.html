What is competitive programming?


Competitive Programming is a mind sport where participants will write programs according to the provided specifications. The provided problems will test participant’s problem solving skills and their ability to apply computer science concepts that they have learned during their university study.

Participants are also implementing their solutions under time pressure and others who are participating in the competition.
Scoreboard will be shown in real-time and will show all participants and problems that they have successfully solved.

Competitive Programming is supported by multinational tech companies such as Google, Facebook, Quora, Shopee, et cetera.
